# 3-Up-3-Down

Based off of the card game

## Three Up Three Down — Quick Rules Cheat Sheet
### Setup

**Each player gets 9 cards**

- 3 face‑down (don’t look)

- 3 face‑up (on top of the face‑down)

- 3 in hand

- Play starts from your hand, then face‑up, then face‑down.

### Gameplay

**Your Turn**

- Play one or more cards of the same rank.

- Your play must be equal to or higher than the top of the discard pile.

- If you cannot play, you pick up the entire pile.

**Special Cards**

- 10 (Clear): Immediately clears the pile; next player can play anything.


### Moving Through Your Cards

**Hand cards**

- Keep drawing from the deck until it’s empty.

**Face‑up cards**

- Once your hand is empty, you must play these.

**Face‑down cards**

- Blind plays. Flip one and hope it works.

- If it doesn’t, pick up the pile.

### Winning

- First player to play all hand, face‑up, and face‑down cards wins.
